About this Project :-

This project aims to provide insights into the various chemical factors that contribute to the quality of red wine, such as acidity, alcohol content, and volatile acidity. By visualizing and understanding these components, it becomes possible to make informed decisions related to production, selection, and improvement of red wines.

Key Insights :-

  • Generally, wine's pH value is found between 3 to 3.5. The average pH value is 3.31.
  • The average alcohol percentage is 10.44%.
  • Wine samples contain a maximum score of 5 or 6.
  • Volatile acidity and total fixed acidity are negatively correlated with each other.
  • Fixed acidity and volatile acidity diverged the most when the quality was 7, with fixed acidity being 8.46 higher than volatile acidity.
  • Across all 87 pH values, quality ranged from 1 to 41.
