About this Project :-

This project analyzes Netflix's (NASDAQ: NFLX) stock performance, examining historical prices, trading volume, and key financial indicators to offer insights for investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in its market performance.

Key Insights :-

  • Stock price increasing with rate of -15.57% in recent year.
  • Average volume rate is 7.95 million.
  • market share in hike till 2021 with maximum closing price of 660k
  • Analyzing historical trends and patterns in Netflix's stock data can help identify potential investment opportunities or risks, enabling informed decision-making for investors and traders.
